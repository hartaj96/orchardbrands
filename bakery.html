Orchard Brands offers premium quality bakery toppings and fillings designed using real fruits and superior quality ingredients. These formulations are specifically for baked goods deliver top-notch flavour and pleasing aroma to your final products at the same time making them visually inviting.

We provide customised solutions for requirements of high baking stability and low water activity products with high fruit content and real fruit bites. Our range of toppings are an excellent choice for deposited or injected post baking application with fruit inclusions, fruit peels, whole fruits and natural colours.
